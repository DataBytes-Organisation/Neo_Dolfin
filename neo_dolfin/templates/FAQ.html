FAQ

DolFin is a Fintech solution that aims to address the issue of people struggling to keep track of their finances, the difficulties and costs associated with obtaining finanical advice, banks acting monopolistically and traditional banking not serving the people equally.

DolFin uses open banking data to display your financial accounts, providing you with high-level summaries, detailed reports, and graphs/visual diagrams on desktop and mobile platforms. Our main goal at DolFin is to help you get on top of your finances.

DolFin also provides customized advice using an AI prediction model and chatbot. The AI prediction model allows for your financial information to be securely analysed to provide savings predictions and comparisions to others in similar situations.

DolFin is designed for both desktop and mobile and can be accessed on these platforms.

DolFin is a completely free and easy way for you to keep track of your finances. Join DolFin today and start saving!
