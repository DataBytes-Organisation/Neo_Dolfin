DolFin is a Fintech solution that aims to address the issue of people struggling to keep track of their finances, the difficulties and costs associated with obtaining finanical advice, banks acting monopolistically and traditional banking not serving the people equally.

DolFin uses open banking data to display your financial accounts, providing you with high-level summaries, detailed reports, and graphs/visual diagrams on desktop and mobile platforms. Our main goal at DolFin is to help you get on top of your finances.

DolFin also provides customized advice using an AI prediction model and chatbot. The AI prediction model allows for your financial information to be securely analysed to provide savings predictions and comparisions to others in similar situations.

DolFin is designed for both desktop and mobile and can be accessed on these platforms.

In the banking sector, consumers may authorize sharing of data such as customer details (name, occupation, contact details, business information), account data (account number, type, balance, interest rates), transaction data (transaction details, amounts, descriptions), and saved payee details.

DolFin is a completely free and easy way for you to keep track of your finances. Join DolFin today and start saving!

DolFin will collect your personal information for the following purposes:

To provide you with the services you have requested.

To improve our services.

To contact you about our services.

To comply with any legal or regulatory requirements.

DolFin uses a variety of security tools to monitor for vulnerabilities and suspicious activity. These tools include: Firewalls, Antivirus software, Intrusion detection systems, Security information and event management (SIEM) systems,

Encryption: All personal information is encrypted when stored on DolFin's servers

Authentication: Users are required to authenticate themselves before they can access their accounts

Malware protection: All traffic is scanned for malicious software using malware protection software

DolFin also monitors system logs for suspicious activity. This includes things like unusual login attempts or access to sensitive data.

DolFin monitors system logs for suspicious activity and upon detection will block the users account, if the user believes their account has been compromised they can contact DolFin at info@dolfin.com.au
